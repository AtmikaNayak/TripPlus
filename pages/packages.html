Duration: 8 Days / 7 Nights | Destinations: Paris, France & Rome, Italy

A complete European city break without the planning stress. Includes central 4-star boutique hotel stays, your flight between the two cities, skip-the-line passes to the Louvre and the Colosseum, and an evening Seine river cruise.

10-Day Bali Island Hopper

Duration: 10 Days / 9 Nights | Destinations: Ubud, Seminyak, Nusa Penida

Your ultimate tropical escape. This comprehensive package features all airport transfers, a 4-night stay in a private Ubud jungle villa, 5 nights at a beachfront resort in Seminyak, and a curated full-day snorkeling excursion.
